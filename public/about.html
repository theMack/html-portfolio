About Me

SOCIAL ENTREPRENEUR

Joe is a performance focused social entrepreneur, who creates projects to benefit underserved communities consistently driven by a desire to address social or environmental issues and make a positive impact on disadvantaged communities. He uses innovative and sustainable approaches to create projects that address the specific needs of underserved populations, such as providing access to capital, education, entrepreneurialship, healthcare, clean water, employment opportunities, and affordable housing. Innovator in launching concepts, ideas, and programs, utilizing sustainable and scalable solutions to address systemic challenges and improve the quality of life for those in underserved communities.

A demonstrated track record of developing three (3) successful farmers' markets; writer of several RFPs and RFIs, grant application, proposals, business plans, to public and private entities - for over a 30 year period; owner and operator of several food related enterprises; conceptualizing and implementing large scale projects; being a technical evangelist; and, for conducting research and case studies on leading edge neighborhood development projects from around the world and making determinations on the probability of implementation within a team effort.
